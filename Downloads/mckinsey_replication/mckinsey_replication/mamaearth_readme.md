# Mamaearth Supply Chain Diagnostic (Desk-Research Replication)

This project applies a structured consulting-style diagnostic to public data for Honasa Consumer (Mamaearth).

## Scope
- Public filings and publicly accessible digital signals
- Five-lever supply chain assessment (demand, visibility, inventory, logistics, digital)
- Scenario-based impact modeling

## Data Transparency Note
- This analysis uses proxy signals (for example, Google Trends) and public company information.
- Proxy signals are directional and should not be treated as equivalent to proprietary transaction datasets used in full consulting engagements.
- Estimates are illustrative and intended for strategic prioritization, not audited financial guidance.

## Positioning
- Correct framing: **desk research replicating consulting methodology**
- Not claimed: identical data depth or execution parity with proprietary consulting data stacks

## Indicative Impact Range
- Modeled upside is presented as a range to reflect uncertainty.
- Current defensible aggregate estimate: **₹280–320 crore** potential improvement range, subject to data quality and implementation assumptions.

## Next Steps
1. Validate assumptions with operating teams and domain practitioners.
2. Replace proxy inputs with first-party operational data where possible.
3. Convert highest-confidence levers into pilot interventions and tracked KPI outcomes.
